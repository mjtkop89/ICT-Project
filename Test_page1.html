Introduction


Buffalo Cricket Club was the brainchild of a few international students here at the University at Buffalo in upstate western New York. Cricket is the most popular sport in the Indian subcontinent and a good percent of the Indian students at UB are from the Indian subcontinent and hence the lack of cricket on the university campus asked for an organization taking care of the needs of the students interested in playing cricket. As a result, the UB Cricket Club aka Buffalo Cricket Club was formed as a sports club under the Student Association of the University at Buffalo in the fall of 2011.
